Privacy Policy for FamilyScheduler

Effective date: 2026-04-05

This Privacy Policy explains how FamilyScheduler ("the App", "we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects user information when you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

When you use FamilyScheduler, we may collect and process the following information:

  • Email address and authentication-related information when you create an account or sign in.
  • User-created content such as family schedules, tasks, reminders, and related app data.
  • Technical information necessary for app functionality, security, and reliability.
  • If notifications are enabled, we may process notification-related identifiers such as a push token in order to deliver reminders and updates.

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage your account.
  • Store and sync your schedules, tasks, and related data.
  • Send reminders and app notifications.
  • Maintain app functionality, security, and reliability.
  • Improve the app experience and fix technical issues.

3. Services We Use

FamilyScheduler uses third-party services provided by Google Firebase, including:

  • Firebase Authentication for account sign-in and account management.
  • Cloud Firestore for storing and syncing app data.
  • Notification-related Firebase services, if enabled, for delivering reminders and updates.

These services may process data as necessary to provide their functionality.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share data with service providers such as Firebase when necessary to operate the app and provide its features.

5. Data Retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the app’s features. If you request deletion of your account or data, we will make reasonable efforts to delete relevant data, unless retention is required for legal, security, or legitimate operational reasons.

6. Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children’s Privacy

FamilyScheduler is not specifically designed for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in violation of applicable law. If you believe such information has been provided, please contact us.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to request access, correction, or deletion of your data. To make such a request, contact us using the email below.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
